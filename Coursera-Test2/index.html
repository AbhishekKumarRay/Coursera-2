INDIAN DISHES

Aloo Tikki

Think of aloo tikki as delicious Indian potato fritters. These appetizers or snacks are made with mashed potatoes and a variety of spices, formed into patties, then fried in oil.

Mango Chutney

This quintessential Indian condiment is used to top many Indian appetizers and is made with mangoes, vinegar, sugar, and spices. Mango chutney is probably the most recognizable chutney.

Samosas

Think of samosas as being an Indian version of empanadas. These are a favorite appetizer made with with a flaky pastry enveloping a hearty filling of potatoes and vegetables.
